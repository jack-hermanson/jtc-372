Using the Plotter

This article shows how to request a refund using the Papercut print credit system. Refunds will take a few days to process.

Step 1

Identify the Papercut window at the top right corner of the window. Click on the "Details..." link.

Step 2

Log into the Papercut system using your ename and password. These are the same credentials you use to log into RamWeb, Canvas, and the CHHS computer lab machines.

Step 3

The left side of the screen will have a dark navigation menu. Click on the "Recent Print Jobs" link. It is the 4th from the bottom.

Step 4

On the Recent Print Jobs screen, you will see a table with all of your recent print jobs. To request a refund, click on the "request refund" link.

Step 5

Once you click on the "request refund" link, you will be taken to a screen where you can enter the amount of the refund and the reason for it. Please enter what happened in the text box, then click submit.

That's it!
