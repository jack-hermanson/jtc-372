RDC 104

Welcome to the RDC 104 computer lab. Located on the first floor of the Nancy Richardson Design Center at 522 W Lake St, directly east of the Stadium, this lab serves students in Apparel Merchandising, Interior Architecture and Design, and IDEA programs.

The RDC 104 lab has 87 computers available to students and a teacher podium with a projector screen.

Reservations

The RDC 104 lab is reserved for classes during the following times:

  • Monday: 8:00am-9:50am, 10:00am-10:50am, 12:00pm-12:50pm, 3:00pm-3:50pm, 4:00pm-4:50pm
  • Tuesday: 12:00pm-12:50pm, 2:00pm-3:40pm, 4:00pm-5:50pm
  • Wednesday: 8:00am-9:50am, 10:00am-10:50am, 12:00pm-12:50pm, 3:00pm-3:50pm, 4:00pm-4:50pm
  • Thursday: 11:00am-12:15pm, 2:00pm-3:40pm
  • Friday: 8:00am-9:50am, 10:00am-11:50am

Hardware

The RDC 104 lab has a color printer, a black and white printer, and a plotter. The computers in the front lab are HP Z2 G4 workstations equipped with 16GB of RAM. The computers in the back lab are HP Z4 G4 workstations equipped with 32GB of RAM.
