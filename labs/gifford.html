Gifford 317

Welcome to the Gifford 317 computer lab. Located on the third floor of the Gifford building at 502 W Lake St, directly east of the Richardson Design Center Building, this lab serves students in Apparel Merchandising, Food Science and Human Nutrition, and Interior Architecture and Design majors.

[add pic of gifford]

The Gifford 317 lab has 99 computers available to students and a teacher podium with a projector screen.

Reservations

The Gifford 317 lab is reserved for classes during the following times:

  • Monday: 9:00am-2:40pm, 3:00pm-5:15pm
  • Tuesday: 9:00am-12:15pm, 12:30pm-6:40pm
  • Wednesday: 9:00am-1:50pm, 3:00pm-5:15pm
  • Thursday: 9:00am-12:15pm, 12:30pm-6:40pm
  • Friday: 10:00am-1:50pm, 2:30pm-4:30pm

Hardware

The Gifford 317 lab has a color printer, two black and white printers, and a plotter. The computers are HP Z2 Mini workstations equipped with 8GB of RAM.
