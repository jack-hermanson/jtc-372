Education 220

Welcome to the Education 220 computer lab. Located on the second floor of the Education building at 450 W Pitkin St, directly west of the Behavioral Sciences Building, this lab serves students in the Education, Social Work, and Human Development and Family Studies majors.

The Education 220 lab has 43 computers available to students and a teacher podium with a projector screen.

Reservations

The Education 220 lab is reserved for classes during the following times:

  • Monday: 10:00am-10:50am, 11:00am-1:50pm
  • Tuesday: 9:00am-10:40am, 2:00pm-2:50pm, 3:00pm-4:50pm
  • Wednesday: 10:00am-10:50am, 7:00pm-8:00pm
  • Thursday: 9:00am-10:40am, 2:00pm-2:50pm
  • Friday: 9:00am-10:50am

Hardware

The Education 220 lab has a color printer, black and white printer, and a plotter. The computers are HP Z2 Mini workstations equipped with 8GB of RAM.

Software

The following software is available in the Education 220 lab:

  • Audacity
  • Autodesk 2020 (3ds Max, Architecture, Arnold, AutoCAD, Inventor, NavisWorks, Revit)
  • EndNote
  • Microsoft Office
  • Microsoft Project
  • Microsoft Visio
  • NVivo
  • Read&Write Gold
  • Rstudio Desktop
  • SAS University
  • SketchUp Pro
  • SMART Notebook
  • SPSS, AMOS
  • V-Ray
  • WindowShades
  • Zotero
