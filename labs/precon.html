Precon 200

Welcome to the Precon 200 computer lab. Located on the second floor of the Preconstruction Center at 251 W Laurel St, north of the Oval and directly south of the Rainbow Caf√©, this lab serves Construction Management and Interior Archiecture and Design majors.

The Precon 200 lab has 95 computers available to students and 2 teacher podiums with projector screens.

Hardware

The Precon 200 lab has a color printer, 2 black and white printers, and a plotter. The computers are HP Z2 Mini workstations equipped with 8GB of RAM.
